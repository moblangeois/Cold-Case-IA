On June 4, 2010, Kyron Horman was 7 years old. The second-grader arrived early to Portland’s Skyline Elementary School with his stepmother, Terri Moulton Horman, to tour a science fair set for that afternoon.

She took a picture of Kyron in front of his poster about tree frogs. It was the last known photograph taken of him.
